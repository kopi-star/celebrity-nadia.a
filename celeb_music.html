HER MUSIC

NIKI is considered an RnB artist and she has 71 songs on spotify. Alongside this, she also has 5 albums. During April 2025, she has able to hit 4.4B streams on the platform. Her most popular song is "Take a Chance With Me" from the album Nicole. While the album was released in 2022, she wrote the song in 2015.

.

Her first album, however, was Moonchild in 2020 which was released under 88rising. The album was a success given that it was considered the best Asian albums of the year by NME. For context, the NME Awards is a music awards show in the UK. Today, Moonchild is still considered her most popular album with 243,821,262 total streams and 156,348 daily streams on Spotify.

PHILANTROPIC WORK

Not only is she an accomplished singer but she also seeks to make change within the music industry. She has advocated for Asian Representation within her industry by showcasing her own culture during her performances and encouraging others to do the same. She's advocated for more representation, for instance, with inviting children on stage to wave flags and pay their respects alongside opening one of her performances wihthe Indonesian National Anthem. Her performances are done with the intent of encouraging other artists to share their own culture.

HER FANS

As seen by her fame on spotify and success as a musician, she's widely known in various countries. While she is based in the United States and is from Indonesia, she also has listeners from the Philippines, Canada, England, Malasyia, and Singapore. Furthermore, she has had concerts in North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.
